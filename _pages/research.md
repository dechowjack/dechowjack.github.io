My research aims to improve our understanding of mountain snowmelt dynamics and develop
better model representations of snow water resources in complex terrain.

Some of my specific research interests include:

- Improving snowmelt model estimates in orographically complex catchments
- Understanding snow-atmosphere interactions and energy balance processes
- Developing scalable methods for snow water equivalent estimation from remote sensing
- Hydrological modeling and uncertainty quantification
